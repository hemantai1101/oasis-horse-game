Your turn
30
Slide (to end) Knight (L-jump)

How to Play

Goal: Be the first to move any squirrel onto the 🌰 Acorn in the center.

Setup: White starts in two opposite corners (top-left & bottom-right). Black takes the other two.

On your turn, move one squirrel:

  • Slide Move in a straight line (up/down/left/right) until you hit the edge or another squirrel. You land on the last empty cell.
  • Knight Jump in an L-shape (like a chess knight), but only to desert cells.

You cannot land on or jump over another squirrel.

This game was played by IVE's Gaeul on Netflix's Death Game. This is a fan tribute in her honor. 🩷
